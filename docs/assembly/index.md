# Assembly Overview

This section walks through the complete assembly of Trashbot.

!!! tip "Before you start"
    - Review the [Bill of Materials](../bom.md) and confirm you have all parts.
    - Download the laser cut files from the [images directory](https://github.com/IRL-CT/Summer25_Trashbot/tree/main/images) before your first session.
    - Tools needed: screwdrivers, drill, soldering iron, wire strippers.

---

## Assembly Steps

Follow the guides in order:

1. [**Frame**](frame.md) — Set up the dolly and attach the laser-cut circular mounting plate
2. [**Electronics & Wiring**](electronics.md) — Mount ODrive, build filtering PCBs, wire motors and Raspberry Pi
3. [**Drive System**](drive.md) — Understand the hoverboard differential drive system
4. [**Sensors & Accessories**](sensors.md) — Mount GoPro and attach the trash bin

---

## Tools Required

- Phillips and flathead screwdrivers
- Power drill (for dolly holes)
- Soldering iron and solder
- Wire strippers
- Multimeter
- Laptop (for SSH and ODrive tool)
- Access to a laser cutter

---
