# Research

The CT-IRL Mobile Platform has been used across multiple published studies on Human-Robot Interaction in public spaces. Below is a summary of the research conducted using this platform, led by [Fanjun (Frank) Bu](https://frankbu0616.github.io/) and the [Cornell Tech Interaction Lab](https://github.com/IRL-CT).

---

## Trash Barrel Robots in the Wild

The platform is best known for the **Trash Barrel Robot** deployments — hoverboard-based mobile bases with trash barrels attached, deployed in real public spaces in New York City using the Wizard of Oz method.
